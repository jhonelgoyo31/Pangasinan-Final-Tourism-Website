Before You Go

Travel Tips

Helpful reminders for safer, smoother, and more respectful travel around Pangasinan.

Best time to visit

Plan beach and island trips during dry months when sea travel is safer and skies are clearer.

Transportation

Prepare route stops ahead of time. Some destinations need tricycle, jeepney, boat, or private vehicle transfers.

Safety reminders

Bring water, sun protection, modest clothing for churches, and always follow local travel advisories.

Where to stay

Choose hotels or homestays near your main route, such as Alaminos for islands or Bolinao for beaches.

Budget & Money Tips

Bring cash for local transport, entrance fees, cottages, boat rentals, and food stalls.

Eco-Tourism & Respect

Keep beaches and farms clean, respect sacred spaces, and support local tourism providers.
